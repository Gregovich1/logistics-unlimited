  • Do You Ship Outside of Arizona?
  • We are now offering delivery to all 50 states. The shipping fees will vary, as will the delivery time. You can rest assured that we will get your order to you as quickly as possible.
  • Do You Ship to Canada?
  • Our customers ordering from Canada are able to make a purchase and request shipping through our website. The order will be processed in U.S. dollars, so you should be aware of the difference in cost after the exchange rate. Orders to Canada are not eligible for free shipping, due to the additional charges related to international shipments. Additionally, you should expect duty charges from your government.
